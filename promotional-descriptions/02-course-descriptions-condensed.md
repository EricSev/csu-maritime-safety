# Four Courses. One Industry-Ready Skillset.

**Version B - Condensed Course Descriptions**

---

CSU's Maritime Safety Concentration prepares professionals with specialized knowledge across four critical areas:

- **Maritime Safety Standards**: Regulatory compliance, emergency response, risk management, and human factors analysis
- **Maritime Workers Compensation**: Jones Act, LHWCA frameworks, claims administration, and litigation procedures
- **Special Topics in Maritime Industry**: Ship design, international standards, cybersecurity, automation, and sustainability challenges
- **Materials Handling**: Cargo operations, port logistics, lifting equipment safety, and load restraint protocols

Developed with Signal Mutual member input, this curriculum bridges academic theory with real-world maritime operations. Available fully online through CSU's accredited platform.
