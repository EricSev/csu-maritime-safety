# Maritime Safety Concentration: Course Overview (Detailed)

**Version A - Course-Focused**

---

Columbia Southern University's Maritime Safety Concentration delivers targeted expertise through four industry-focused courses. **Maritime Safety Standards** covers international and domestic regulations, risk management, emergency response, and accident investigation. **Introduction to Maritime Workers Compensation** explores the Jones Act, LHWCA, claims processing, and settlement procedures. **Special Topics in Maritime Industry** examines ship construction principles, emerging technologies, automation, cybersecurity, and environmental sustainability. **Materials Handling in Maritime Industry** addresses cargo handling operations, port facility layouts, lifting equipment, and load restraint systems. Each course is designed with input from maritime employers to ensure curriculum relevance and practical applicability.
